About FakeJobAI

Empowering job seekers with Artificial Intelligence to identify fraudulent job postings in real-time.

Our Mission

The job market is flooded with scams. Our mission is to build a safer employment ecosystem by providing instant, data-driven verification of job listings. We use advanced NLP to analyze patterns that human eyes might miss, protecting your time, data, and money.

The Vision

We envision a world where every job application is secure. By combining community reporting with Machine Learning, FakeJobAI aims to become the global standard for employment verification, integrating directly with major job boards and recruitment platforms.

⚙️ How the AI Works

1. Data Ingestion

We extract text from URLs or process your uploaded CSV/Manual entry.

2. Preprocessing

Text is cleaned, tokenized, and vectorized using TF-IDF to extract meaningful features.

3. Prediction

Our trained Classifier analyzes key phrases (e.g., "urgent", "wire transfer").

4. Verification

Results are cross-referenced with company profiles and domain metadata.
